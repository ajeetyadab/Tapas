Privacy Policy

Tapas Study Tracker

Last updated: June 7, 2026

👋 Overview

Tapas Study Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application for iOS and Android that helps you track study sessions and build consistent learning habits.

This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it. We believe in keeping things simple and transparent.

📋 Information We Collect

Account information

  • Email address (used for login and account recovery)
  • Display name (optional, set by you)

Study & usage data

  • Study sessions you log (subject, duration, date)
  • Goals and streaks you set within the app
  • In-app actions and feature usage (to improve the product)
  • App crash reports and performance data
Note: We do not collect your name, phone number, payment information, location, contacts, or any sensitive personal data.

🎯 How We Use Your Data

Purpose Data used
Provide & sync your account Email, study data
Send account-related emails (password reset, etc.) Email address
Display your progress and streaks Study sessions, goals
Improve app performance and fix bugs Crash reports, usage events
Analyze feature usage to improve the product Anonymized usage analytics

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

🔗 Third-Party Services

We use the following Google services to operate the app:

  • Firebase Authentication — manages secure sign-in and account sessions. Firebase Privacy Policy ↗
  • Firebase Firestore — stores your study data in the cloud so it syncs across devices.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — collects anonymized crash reports to help us fix bugs faster.
  • Google Analytics for Firebase — tracks anonymized in-app events (screens viewed, features used) to help us understand how people use the app. Google Privacy Policy ↗

Data processed by these services is subject to Google's privacy policies. You can opt out of analytics data collection in the app settings.

🗂️ Data Retention

Your account data and study records are retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days.

Anonymized analytics data may be retained longer for aggregate reporting purposes and cannot be linked back to you.

🛡️ Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion — delete your account and all associated data from within the app settings, or by emailing us
  • Opt out — disable analytics tracking in app settings
  • Portability — request an export of your study data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at ajeetyadax@gmail.com.

👶 Children's Privacy

Tapas Study Tracker is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU/UK). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

🔒 Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase infrastructure, which uses industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. We follow security best practices and restrict access to your data to authorized systems only.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. If you discover a security issue, please report it to ajeetyadax@gmail.com.

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we'll notify you via the app or by email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
